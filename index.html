Ice paradise is a one-stop shop for ice cream. Our ice creams are made with 100% naturally sourced Canadian ingredients. There are no preservatives, sugar or artificial flavors added. We have a menu curated to create a mouth watering experience for everyone. There are dairy free, glutten free and nut-free options available. We are located in Calgary, Alberta. We have four locations in total. Visit us to enjoy some sweet treats!
